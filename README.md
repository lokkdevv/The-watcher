# The Watcher
A survival horror game built with **C** and **raylib**.
You are an eye in the dark whose light is too powerfull and hurts humans, so they try to kill you while you have to defend yourself against more and more advanced humans.

---
## Gameplay
- Hold your gaze at a human long enough to burn them
- Stare to send them fleeing
- Survive the waves as they grow larger and more coordinated
## Enemy Types
- **Villager** Runs if you stare at them and easy to scatter.
- **Hunter** Deals more damage than the villager and takes sustained staring to repel.
- **Priest** Holds a lantern that shrinks your effective gaze range and makes you slower.
- **Scientist** Hangs back and never attacks, if left alive too long he buffs all other humans on screen.
---
## Building
### Requirements
- [raylib 4.5+](https://github.com/raysan5/raylib)
- GCC or Clang
- Make
### Windows
```bash
make
```
